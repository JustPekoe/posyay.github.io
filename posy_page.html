Oray 1.0

Project

Oray 1.0 is a praying robot. The purpose of Oray is to pray for anything. Oray is able to do the praying motion and output text on a crystal liquid display. All of Oray's actions are powered by the Arduino Uno ciruit board and programmed with Arduino.
